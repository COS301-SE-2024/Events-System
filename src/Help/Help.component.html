FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to questions you may have about SynC and the Events system
Can't find what you're looking for? Check out our full User Manual.

I do not have the pages for creating events?

The ability to create events, series and hosts, is limited to only hosts, if you signed up to just attend events, you will not have this feature.

If, however, you did sign up as a host, and still cannot see this feature, please send an email to bieber.capstone@gmail.com for assistance

Can the AI help me plan virtual events?

Eve is our events assistant, she is limited to event related activity, like RSVPing to events, finding preperation details for an event, leaving a review, and many more event related actions!.

How do I create an event in the system?

A: Events can only be created by hosts, so if you happen to be a host! Creating an event in our system is a straightforward process that involves filling out a form on our website and submitting it. Here's how you can do it:

  • 1. Navigate to the Event creation page.
  • 2. Fill Out the Event Form: On the event creation page, you will see a form that asks for details about the event you want to create. Fill out all the required fields, which typically include the event name, date, time, location, and a brief description.
  • 3. Submit the Form: After filling out the form, review the information to make sure everything is correct. Then, click the "Submit" button to create your event.
  • 4. Confirmation: Once your event is successfully created, you will receive a confirmation pop up. This indicates that your event has been added to our system and is now visible to other users!
Where do I look up events. I couldn't find a search bar

To access the search functionality, use the search icon located in the top navbar, on the left of your profile picture, or to the right of the notifications bell!

Alternatively, open the menu and navigate to the search page

Can I edit an event after creating it?

A: Yes, you can edit an event after it has been created. To do this, navigate to the My Events page and look for the "Edit" button. Clicking this button will allow you to update the event's details.

Where can i communicate with the event organizers on the app?

The app does not directly support messaging, but you can communicate with the event organizer, by navigating to the events page, and clicking on the hosts name, this will direct you to the hosts profile, where you can view their social media links

How do I see who has RSVP'd for my event?

To find out who has RSVPd to your event, navigate to the My Events page and click on the check in button in the event. You will see a list of attendees who have RSVPd to the event.

Once you're in this page, you can view more information about each attendee or remove them from the event.

Can this app be used on mobile too?

Yes! the app can be viewed on both desktop and mobile devices, use the "Download App" option in the menu, to download it to your device!

What is a series?

in short: A series a short term collection of events that fall under a specific topic or theme, like social clubs, but not persistent, once the final event in the series has taken place, the series is no longer accessible!

Find more of our series here

does history in the schedule page show me only my event history or all events that have taken place?

The history tab in the My Schedule page shows only events that you have RSVPd to, and have taken place, it does not show all events that have taken place.

How can I create a new event for an existing series?

Once you have created a series, feel free to create new events, and use or "edit" option in the My series page to add the new event to the existing series!

Is there a limit to the number of events companies can create?

There is no limit! shoot for the stars, and get to the top of the leaderboard!

Is my data secure on the app?

We do our best to ensure the protection and security of your data, by minimizing the sensitive data we collect, and the parties that can access it

Find out more in our Privacy policy

How do I contact the app support team?

If you encounter technical issues, or need to contact support, reach out to use at bieber.capstone@gmail.com

How do I set a reminder for an event?

Signing up to our app using google will automatically sync your events to your google calendar, and you will receive reminders for your events, a day, and an hour before the event starts

Is there a way to quickly check if I have booked an event without having to enter the event details

Yes! The Upcoming events section of the homepage, shows all your upcoming events in chronological order, feel free to take a tour of the home page to find out where!

My account

  • Update Account Details:
    • Navigate to your profile icon at the top right corner and select Settings from the dropdown menu.
    • In the settings menu, click on My Details to access and edit your personal information such as name, surname, and description.
    • Remember to save your changes before exiting.
  • Privacy Settings:
    • Go to Settings by clicking on your profile icon.
    • Select Security from the sidebar.
    • Here, you can toggle the visibility settings for your surname and account links.
    • Choose your preferred level of privacy and click Save Changes to apply.
  • Change Description:
    • Update your profile description by accessing Settings > My Details.
    • Scroll down to the Description section, where you can edit your professional bio.
    • Use the text box to add or modify your description.
    • Click Save Changes to update your profile.
  • Add a Profile Photo:
    • To upload a new profile photo, visit Settings > Profile Picture.
    • Click on the Choose File button and select an image from your device.
    • Supported formats are JPG, PNG, and GIF. Adjust the cropping if needed.
    • Click Save Changes to set your new profile picture.
  • Change Password:
    • For security, regularly update your password under Settings > Security.
    • Click on Change Password, enter your current password, and then your new password twice for confirmation.
    • Passwords should be at least 8 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
    • Click Save Changes to complete the change.
  • Delete Account:
    • If you decide to delete your account, be aware that this action is irreversible and will result in the loss of all your data.
    • To proceed, navigate to Settings > Security, scroll to the bottom of the page, and click on Delete Account.
